# Negative Prompts
# Copy these into your image generation tool's negative prompt field.
# Do not include in the positive prompt.

# --- Anatomy & Limbs ---
deformed paws, malformed paws, extra paws, missing paws, fused paws
deformed hands, malformed hands, extra fingers, missing fingers, fused fingers
bad hands, ugly hands, mutated hands, poorly drawn hands
extra limbs, missing limbs, floating limbs, disconnected limbs
bad anatomy, wrong anatomy, mutated anatomy
extra tails, extra ears, extra eyes, extra heads
asymmetric face, asymmetric ears, lopsided features

# --- Face & Head ---
ugly face, deformed face, distorted face, poorly drawn face
cross-eyed, wall-eyed, misaligned eyes
open mouth showing teeth (unless specified), drooling
blurry face, low detail face

# --- Quality & Artifacts ---
blurry, out of focus, soft focus, low resolution, low quality
jpeg artifacts, compression artifacts, pixelated, grainy, noisy
watermark, text, signature, logo, stamp, username, caption
border, frame, vignette (unless intentional)
oversaturated, washed out, flat lighting

# --- Style Violations ---
modern clothing, western clothing, jeans, t-shirts, sneakers
guns, modern weapons, mechanical objects
cartoon style, anime style, chibi (unless intentional)
photorealistic (for ink wash / ukiyo-e styles)
CGI, 3D render, digital painting (for traditional styles)

# --- Composition ---
multiple animals in frame, crowd, background characters
duplicate subject, mirror image artifacts
cluttered composition, busy background that overwhelms subject
