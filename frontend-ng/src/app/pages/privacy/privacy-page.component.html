Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Pet-to AI is a product of Nakama AI. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use Pet-to AI ("Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information — When you sign in via Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Firebase Authentication.
  • Uploaded images — Photos of pets you upload to generate portraits.
  • Order information — Shipping address and contact details you provide when placing a print order.
  • Usage data — Basic information about how you interact with the Service (e.g. generations requested, styles selected).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI portraits from your uploaded images.
  • To process and fulfil print orders.
  • To authenticate you and manage your account.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To improve the Service.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party providers to operate the Service. Each is governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and data storage.
  • Razorpay — Payment processing. We do not store your card details.
  • RunPod — AI image generation infrastructure. Uploaded images are transmitted to RunPod solely to generate your portrait and are not retained by them beyond processing.
  • Google Cloud — Hosting and infrastructure.

5. Data Retention

Uploaded images are stored temporarily and used only to generate your portrait. Generated portraits are retained in your account gallery until you delete them. Order information is retained as long as necessary to fulfil and support your order. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Information is shared only as necessary to deliver the Service (e.g. shipping address with our print fulfilment partner when you place a print order).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@nakamaai.co. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted means you accept the revised policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Reach out to us at contact@nakamaai.co.
